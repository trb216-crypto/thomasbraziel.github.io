M&G Chemicals Mechanic’s Liens (3x+ Return)

In 2017, we acquired mechanic’s lien claims in the M&G USA Corp bankruptcy for approximately 30% of face value. These liens were secured under Texas constitutional law and sat high in the capital structure.
