BitClave Advisory Engagement

In early 2025, I was brought in by a group of BitClave ($CAT) ICO claimants facing near-total losses. Their tokens were illiquid, the claims process was opaque, and most had written off their positions as worthless.

Over several weeks, I worked with the group to reconstruct their pooled token holdings from fragmented wallets, incomplete exchange records, and Telegram archives. We coordinated dozens of claimants globally — many of whom had no formal records — and rebuilt a full proof package for submission.

  • Verified token vesting schedules & chain provenance
  • Consolidated investor positions held across exchanges and cold wallets
  • Negotiated the claim structure directly with estate counsel
  • Protected the group from dilution, rejection, or misallocation

What started as “zero expected recovery” now looks like a win: over $2 million in distributions are expected for the group — from nothing.

“We assumed we were getting zero. This was dead money. But thanks to Tom and his team, we now have real recovery on the table.”
— BitClave claimant (anonymous)
